About Me

Hailing from Louisville Kentucky, I spent my formative years between Oldham and Jefferson counties graduating from South Oldham high school. From there I spent time in the logistics and service industries where I met the future mother of my children Brooke. We had our son and moved to Lexington Kentucky in 2013 where not long after I attended Sullivan University for their Cyber Security Administration program. Nearing the completion of the program I began to work in support roles in the IT industry and am now looking to expand from support to development.
